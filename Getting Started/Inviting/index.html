Getting Started ➜ Inviting

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

To invite Polaris to your server you need to visit our Website and click on the Add To Discord button. Once clicked it will open a prompt from discord whenre you can choose a server to add the bot too. Once the server is selected, give the bot the necesarry permissions and click authorize.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Once authorized you can check in your server if the bot was added. It is recommended to give the bot the highest role and all permissions for full functionality. You can verify if your bot has been correctly invited by running /ping. If the bot was correctly invited, the bot should respond with a ping embed message! If not, please join our Support Server!
